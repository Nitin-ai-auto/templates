Common Questions

Is it really free?

Yes! Our expense tracking features are completely free. We make money on optional premium services like business formation and advanced tax filings.

How secure is my bank data?

Extremely secure. We use Plaid, the industry standard for bank connections, used by apps like Venmo and Robinhood. We never store your bank login credentials.

Can I export to QuickBooks?

Yes, you can export your data to CSV or Excel formats that are compatible with QuickBooks and other accounting software.

Does it work for multiple businesses?

Yes, you can manage expenses for multiple businesses or entities under a single account.
