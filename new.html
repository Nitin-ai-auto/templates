Why use Otto AI invoicing software?

Can I track if an invoice has been paid?



Yes. Otto AI helps you monitor invoice status and sends automatic reminders, so you’ll always know what’s paid, pending, or overdue.

What payment methods do you support?



Otto AI supports bank transfers, credit/debit cards, PayPal, Venmo, Zelle and more. Simply enable your preferred options and we placed one-click payment buttons on every invoice.

Is there an invoice software with automatic calculations?



Yes. Otto AI' free invoicing software auto-calculates totals, taxes, and discounts in real-time, saving you time and avoiding costly mistakes when you managing your invoices.

What should be included in an invoice?



Invoice should include key details like an invoice number, date, your contact information, a brief description of the service, payment terms, due date, and accepted payment methods. Using a free invoicing software helps ensure all necessary details are included.

Should I include taxes on my invoice?



If you're required to charge sales tax or VAT, make sure to include them on your invoice. Otto AI free invoicing software automatically calculates taxes, making your invoicing process smoother and more compliant with tax regulations.

Can I save client info for future invoices?



Yes. Otto AI securely stores client details, so creating future invoices takes seconds, just select the client and everything auto-fills.

Is the free invoicing software customizable?



Yes. Otto AI lets you fully customize invoices, add your logo, colors, currency, taxes, and more, so every invoice fits your brand and client’s needs.
